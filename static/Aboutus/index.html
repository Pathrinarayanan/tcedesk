ABOUT US

This system aims to provide platform where senior, junior and alumni can interact between them.

MISSION

To have a senior - junior interaction panel where students can post their queries and senior can answer them.To display all the details about events,workshops or any announcements.To make the students able to post their queries anonymously.To make the students able to have 1 - 1 interaction with seniors and alumni.

VISION

To Achieve a healthy relationship among the students that build a co-ordination,dedication and sincerity.To guide the students of our college by a web application that contains all the answers to the queries that Students may have.By this, they can have a lasting impact on the student experience.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Initially the application is active for it department,Students are using it everyday especially the freshers as they experience a new environment,the answers are posted without any delay and the system is fuctioning well, soon it will be implemented all over the college based on the feedback given by you.
